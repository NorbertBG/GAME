Life after Iron!

Get ready to join the graduating class of Ironhack in a game that will put your decision-making skills to the test! Choose your player and navigate through a series of tricky questions that will determine their fate. Will they graduate with flying colors, or will they crash and burn? It's up to you to make the tough choices and see where the journey takes you. With plenty of laughs and unexpected twists along the way, this game is sure to be a wild ride. So buckle up, and let's get started!
